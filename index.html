Four independent modules — each designed to do one job perfectly.

Applies DPI bypass to domains blocked in Turkey.

  • Network Analysis — Quick or Deep Scan + mutation engine

Tor Discord

Isolated Discord client over its own Tor tunnel.

  • New account setup — store credentials locally only

Compares your installation against original server files and repairs.
